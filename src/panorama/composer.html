Controls

Rotate the camera: Click-and-drag, or press the arrow keys to make big jumps. You can also hold Shift to make smaller jumps.

Zoom: use the scroll wheel. You can also CTRL and either drag up and down, or use the up and down arrow keys. Hold Shift to zoom faster.

Hold on to an image: Click the camera button, or press Enter, or double click on the vehicle.

Download held images: Click the download button. Images will be saved to your downloads folder, inside this vehicle's folder.
