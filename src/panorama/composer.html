Controls

Rotate the camera: Click-and-drag, or press the arrow keys to make big jumps, or press the arrow keys while holding Shift to make smaller jumps.

Zoom: use the scroll wheel, or click-and-drag while holding CTRL. Hold Shift to zoom faster.

Hold on to an image: Click the camera button, or press Enter, or double click on the vehicle.

Download held images: Click the download button. Images will be saved to your downloads folder, inside this vehicle's folder.
